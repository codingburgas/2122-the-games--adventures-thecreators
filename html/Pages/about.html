This is Zoynyan. He is 300 years old elf who is fighting for his freedom against the evil aliens. They have 19 arms and all of them are full of different types of weapons. He has to gather weapons for range shooting to kill all of them. If he is 1 meter close to them, they will grab him and eat him. You have only 10 hearts to beat 100 rooms full of aliens. Can you do it, are you good enough to fight these monsters? We will see. And remember. His revenge is in your hands.
